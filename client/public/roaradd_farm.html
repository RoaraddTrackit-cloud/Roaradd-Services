The Science

One roof. One loop.
Zero waste.

1
Mushrooms Breathe Out
Oyster, Lion's Mane, and Shiitake fruiting rooms produce excess CO₂. Instead of venting it, we capture every molecule and put it to work.
+CO₂ capture
2
HEPA-Filtered Transfer
CO₂-rich air passes through HEPA H13 + UVC sterilization before routing to the microgreens zone — eliminating all spore contamination risk.
biosecurity maintained
3
Microgreens Feast
Elevated CO₂ (1,200–1,500 ppm target) boosts photosynthesis by 15–20%. Microgreens grow faster and denser with higher nutritional value — zero chemical inputs.
+15–20% yield boost
4
Solar Closes the Loop
A 45kW roof-mounted array with 100kWh battery storage powers every system 24/7. The walk-in cooler pre-cools during peak solar hours — acting as a thermal battery.
net-zero energy Year 2
